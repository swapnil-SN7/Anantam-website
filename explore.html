Earth

Our homeworld is the densest of the eight planets in the Solar System. It is also the largest of the four terrestrial planets.
About 71% of the Earths surface is covered by salt water oceans, and the remaining 30% is taken up by the seven continental landmasses.
Earths outer surface is also divided into several rigid segments, or tectonic plates, that migrate across the surface over periods of millions of years.
